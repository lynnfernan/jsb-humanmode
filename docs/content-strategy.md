# Content Strategy: Evergreen, Pre-Launch & Sustained

> **Philosophy:** Every piece of content either builds the audience, deepens trust, or drives a conversion. Nothing published without a purpose.

---

## Content Architecture

### The Content Pyramid

```
                    [BOOK]
                  /         \
           [SUBSTACK]     [SPEAKING]
          /     |      \
    [SOCIAL]  [EMAIL]  [PODCAST/MEDIA]
         \       |       /
              [SEO / BLOG]
                  |
           [LEAD MAGNET / QUIZ]
```

The quiz and lead magnet sit at the foundation — they convert passive attention into owned audience. All content at higher levels drives traffic back to the foundation.

## Content Pillars (5 Recurring Themes)

| Pillar | Core Question | Content Angle |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Human Leadership** | How do I lead when machines are managing? | Stories, frameworks, decision-making models |
| **Authentic Presence** | Why does my humanity matter now more than ever? | Vulnerability, identity, showing up as yourself |
| **Decision Intelligence** | How do I make better calls when data overwhelms? | Mental models, judgment, wisdom vs. information |
| **Connection at Scale** | How do I build real relationships in a digital world? | Networking, depth vs. breadth, trust-building |
| **Future-Proof Identity** | Who do I need to become to stay relevant? | Reinvention, adaptation, values-anchored growth |

Rotate through all 5 pillars each week across channels.

## Pre-Awareness Campaign (Weeks 3–8)

**Goal:** Build list to 1,000+ before launch. Establish JSB as the definitive voice on human performance in the AI era.

### Week-by-Week Content Calendar

**Week 3 — "The Hook"**
- Substack: Cornerstone post #1 (Human Mode Framework)
- LinkedIn: 3 posts — one story, one insight, one question
- Instagram: Quote graphic from Cornerstone post
- Email: Announce Substack to existing list, invite quiz

**Week 4 — "The Evidence"**
- Substack: "5 Things AI Will Never Do"
- LinkedIn: Share Substack post + personal reflection
- Instagram: 3-panel carousel from the post
- Email: Teaser — "What I've been writing about" + Substack link

**Week 5 — "The Community"**
- Substack: "A Letter to Leaders Who Feel Behind"
- LinkedIn: Start the conversation — ask followers to share their biggest tension with technology
- Instagram: Engagement story — poll, question sticker
- Email: "I have to tell you something" — soft book tease
  - Apply tag `book-launch-active` to all who click

**Week 6 — "The Proof"**
- Substack: Case story (anonymized) — leader who applied Human Mode principles
- LinkedIn: Share early endorsement or reader reaction
- Instagram: Quote from early reader or trusted voice
- Email: Early access offer — "I'm looking for 50 readers"

**Week 7 — "The Stakes"**
- Substack: "Why This Matters More Than Ever Right Now"
- LinkedIn: Industry-specific angle (tailor to JSB's core audience sector)
- Instagram: Behind-the-scenes — JSB writing, recording, or speaking
- Email: "What's inside the book" — reveal one key idea

**Week 8 — "The Countdown Begins"**
- Substack: "The Book Is Almost Here"
- LinkedIn: Announce launch date + pre-order link (if available)
- Instagram: Cover reveal (if timed here) or announcement graphic
- Email: Begin Sequence 2 — Pre-Launch emails

## Launch Campaign (Weeks 9–14)

### Launch Week Content Burst (Week 9)

**Monday (Launch Day):**
- Email: Launch day sequence E1 — "It's here"
- LinkedIn: Long-form launch post (personal story + what the book is about)
- Instagram: Cover graphic + caption
- Substack: "Why I Wrote Human Mode" — most personal post of the campaign

**Tuesday–Thursday:**
- Email: Continued launch sequence
- LinkedIn: One post per day — insight from the book, quote, reader reaction
- Instagram: Stories — real-time reactions, screenshots, shares

**Friday:**
- Email: "48 hours left at launch pricing"
- LinkedIn: "Thank you" post + share the milestone
- Instagram: Community celebration content

### Post-Launch Weeks (Weeks 10–14)

- Substack: 2x/week (one insight-driven, one story-driven)
- LinkedIn: 3x/week
- Instagram: 4x/week
- Email: Weekly newsletter (not a sequence — ongoing broadcast)
- PR: Media appearances, podcast interviews (coordinate with publicist)

## Sustained / Evergreen Content Engine (Week 15+)

### Weekly Rhythm

| Day | Channel | Content Type |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Monday | LinkedIn | Weekly insight post (one idea from the book or JSB's practice) |
| Tuesday | Substack | Feature essay (800–1,500 words, one pillar topic) |
| Wednesday | Instagram | Visual/quote from Tuesday's Substack |
| Thursday | LinkedIn | Story or behind-the-scenes post |
| Friday | Email | Weekly newsletter: curated insight + CTA |
| Saturday | Instagram | Community engagement (question, poll, UGC share) |
| Sunday | — | Prep / ideation only |

### AI-Powered Content Workflow

**Step 1 — Ideation (AI-assisted)**
- Weekly prompt to Claude/GPT: "Based on [pillar], what are 5 specific angles JSB could take given his book's framework and current news/trends?"
- JSB selects 1–2 angles, adds his POV

**Step 2 — Drafting (AI-assisted)**
- Claude/GPT drafts LinkedIn post, email, and Substack intro
- JSB edits for voice (typically 20–30% rewrite)

**Step 3 — Scheduling**
- Approved content loaded to Buffer or Later (social)
- Emails scheduled in CRM
- Substack scheduled in Substack

**Step 4 — Performance Review (Weekly)**
- Check dashboard: which posts drove quiz completions, email sign-ups, or book clicks
- Double down on what's working, cut what's not
- Monthly: Refresh evergreen content based on top performers

## Content Repurposing Matrix

| Source | Repurposed As |
|--------|--------------|
| Substack essay | LinkedIn post (excerpt) + Instagram carousel + email teaser |
| Podcast appearance | Instagram audiogram + LinkedIn quote post + Substack recap |
| Speaking clip | Instagram Reel + LinkedIn video + YouTube short |
| Email response (from subscribers) | Future Substack topic, social post, or book testimonial |
| Book chapter | 5+ LinkedIn posts + 2+ Substack essays + 1 lead magnet |

**Rule:** Every major piece of content gets at least 3 repurposings before retirement.
